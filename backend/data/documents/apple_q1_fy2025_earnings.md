# Apple Inc. — Q1 FY2025 Earnings Report

## Financial Highlights

Apple reported quarterly revenue of $124.3 billion, up 4% year over year and an
all-time record for the December quarter. Diluted earnings per share were $2.40,
up 10% year over year. The company set an all-time revenue record in Services and
in several international markets.

## Services Momentum

Services revenue reached an all-time high of $26.3 billion, up 14% year over year.
Management highlighted record performance across the App Store, cloud services,
advertising, and payment services. The installed base of active devices reached a
new all-time high across all product categories and geographic segments, which the
company described as the foundation of its Services growth.

## iPhone and Hardware

iPhone revenue was $69.1 billion. Mac revenue grew 16% to $9.0 billion, supported
by the latest Apple silicon lineup, and iPad revenue grew 15% to $8.1 billion.
Wearables, Home and Accessories revenue was $11.7 billion.

## Apple Intelligence and AI Strategy

Management discussed the rollout of Apple Intelligence, the company's personal
intelligence system, noting that markets where the features launched saw stronger
iPhone performance than markets where they had not yet launched. The company framed
on-device AI and privacy-preserving intelligence as a central pillar of its product
roadmap and said additional languages and features would expand availability through
the year.

## Gross Margin and Operating Performance

Gross margin was 46.9%, near the high end of guidance, driven by a favorable mix
shift toward Services. Operating cash flow was a record $29.9 billion for the
quarter.

## Capital Return and Outlook

Apple returned over $30 billion to shareholders during the quarter through
dividends and buybacks. For the next quarter, management guided to low to
mid-single-digit revenue growth year over year, with Services expected to continue
growing at a double-digit pace and gross margin guided to a range of 46.5% to 47.5%.
